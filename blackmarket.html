Market Instructions

Trade in your mission tokens for resource points tokens ($RP) at the blackmarket. The black market merchant ONLY accepts the mission token from the previous month's winning team. Players have until the last day of each month to make their exchange. Once a new month kicks in, there's no guarantee they'll be accepted. Exchange rates varies month to month and the merchant only sells whole units of $RP.
