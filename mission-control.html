Mission Instructions

It's time to deploy your $SHIPs on missions! Each month, a new mission will be unveiled in the form of either a PvE or PvP challenge. To participate, players will exchange their $SHIP tokens for a unique mission token (e.g., Mission 1 = $M1, Mission 2 = $M2, etc.) as proof of their involvement. For PvE missions, all players will work together to meet a specific target (e.g., 500 $SHIPs committed before the end of the month) in order to unlock rewards. In contrast, PvP missions will feature multiple teams (each represented by a different mission token) for players to contribute their $SHIPs to. Only the teams that meet the designated threshold requirement will be eligible for rewards.

Collecting rewards

The Proof of Participation system rewards players based on the size of their share of mission tokens. The larger the share, the greater the number of reward tokens that can be earned if the team successfully meets the mission requirements. Players who hold mission tokens that contribute to a successful mission can redeem them for Resource Points ($RP) at the black market.
